Liza is a graduate of UC San Diego where she studied neuroscience.

From there, she worked as a medical assistant for some time before being accepted into Albany Medical College where she completed her M.D.

Currently she is working as a pediatrics resident at Darthmouth-Hitchcock Medical Center, and hopes to land a fellowship in adolescent medicine upon completion of residency in June 2023.

Kyle is a graduate of SUNY Albany where he dual-majored in physics and mathematics. From there he attended Clarkson University where he earned a Master of Arts in Teaching.

Kyle worked for six years as a high school science teacher before leaving the field to pursue a new career.

Kyle is currently self-studying programming and web development in hopes of landing a position when he and Liza relocate to the west coast.

Chanel (Nelly) is a beautiful orange tabby with half a tail.

A rescue from the streets of San Diego, she was adopted by Liza at one year of age after losing her tail in a suspected car accident while raising a litter of kittens on her own.

Nelly loves people but has little tolerance for other cats.

Aramis (Moose) is a fabulous flame-point Siamese with a heart of gold. Also a rescue from San Diego, this big boy spent only a day in the shelter before Liza scooped him up.

While not the most intelligent creature on the planet, and often prone to rapid mood swings, this dopey boy just wants to be petted and loved.

While Moose enjoys the company of Nelly, Nelly would prefer that he not exist.
